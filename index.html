Ruby in Common

Bringing Rubyists together around evolving app approaches.

A new unconf in Sydney 🇦🇺

Let’s gather in Sydney for a day of sharing new and evolving approaches to app building with Ruby!

If you’re interested in Ruby’s greater diversity of tools (such as Hanami, dry-rb, ROM, Literal, Trailblazer, Packwerk, Roda, Phlex, Bridgetown and more), this is the place for you!

We’ll run the day in unconference style. Together, we’ll bring proposals for talks (informal is fine!), discussion topics, hack sessions and the like, then plan an agenda from there. We’ll create an experience tailored to the group’s own interests. You decide what will happen!

The day will be hosted by me, Tim Riley. I can’t wait to see you there! 👋🏼

🗓 When
10th April 2024 (the day before RubyConf AU), 10am-5pm
📍 Where
Microsoft Reactor Sydney, Level 10, 11 York Street
👯 Who
Rubyists of all stripes. You. Come willing to share and participate.
🧘 Code of conduct
Ruby in Common is a harassment-free experience for everyone. Bring positive vibes and a curious mind. See full code of conduct.
